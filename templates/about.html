About Us

At Threat Shield, we believe in a safer digital world for everyone. Our mission is to use advanced AI to detect and neutralize online threats like spam and harassment in texts, images, and links.

Founded by tech visionaries Varsaa H and Kanishka T, we build intelligent, user-first safety systems that protect your digital space.

"Empowering users. Eliminating threats."

Our Story

Threat Shield was founded in 2025 by Varsaa and Kanishka to tackle the growing issues of online abuse and harmful content. Frustrated by weak security tools, they built a powerful platform to scan and safeguard users across text, images, and links.

  • ✔ Text Analysis: Detect spam and harassment in written communications.
  • ✔ Image Recognition: Identify inappropriate or harmful imagery.
  • ✔ URL Scanning: Evaluate links for potential threats and malicious content.

Our Values

  • ✔ Integrity: Transparency and ethics in everything we do.
  • ✔ Innovation: Constantly evolving to tackle new digital threats.
  • ✔ User Empowerment: Giving users control to protect themselves online.
